Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the fly is?

Travel time from Guatemala, México, United States, Canadá, Spain, Germany, South Africa, Japan to Turkmenistan depends on the route, stopovers and available airlines, as there are no direct flights. Generally, it takes at least 24 to 40 hours of travel, including stopovers.

How do I get to the Darvaza Gas Crater?

The crater is about 4-5 hours by car from Ashgabat. Most visitors go by 4x4 tours or private drivers, as the terrain is rough.

Do I need a visa to visit Turkmenistan?

Yes, most travelers require a visa. You’ll need an invitation letter from a tour company.

Is there public transportation to the crater?

No, there are no buses or trains to Darvaza. The only way to get there is by private vehicle, tour, or taxi.

Can I visit the crater at night?

Yes! The crater is most impressive at night when the flames glow brightly. Many tours offer overnight camping nearby, We attached one here in the web.

Is it safe to visit the Darvaza Gas Crater?

Yes, but visitors should stay at a safe distance from the edge since the ground is unstable.

Are there restrooms at the Darvaza Gas Crater?

No, there are no facilities. Be prepared for basic camping conditions..

Is there anything else to see near the crater?

Yes, there are other gas craters nearby (mud and water craters), as well as the Karakum Desert dunes.
