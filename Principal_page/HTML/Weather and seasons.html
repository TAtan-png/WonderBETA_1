Weather and seasons

Turkmenistan has an extreme continental climate, with very hot summers and cold, dry winters.

Spring (March-May): Pleasant climate, temperatures from 10°C to 30°C, some rain.

Summer (June-August): Very hot and dry, between 30°C and 45°C, with maximum temperatures that can exceed 50°C in the desert.

Autumn (September-November): Pleasant temperatures (15°C - 30°C), dry and stable climate.

Winter (December-February): Cold and dry, between -5°C and 10°C, with possible snowfall in the north and mountainous areas.

The best times to visit are spring and autumn, when the weather is milder and more pleasant.
